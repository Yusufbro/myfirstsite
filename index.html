GET FIT or DIE TRYING

Health is an investment, not an Expense ! GET FIT is NOT a commercial gym or a CrossFit box. It's no Basic Fit, Mc Fit or a country club either. GET FIT is a privately owned-and-operated facility for anyone looking to improve, maintain, or increase their fitness at their own pace on their own schedule. We are here to help you in your journey to be better you.




Daily Workouts


Burpees, squats, pull-ups, deadlifts...It's really up to you. We have two core groups of members. One prefers more weightlifting and less cardio. The other prefers more cardio and less weightlifting training. Some members combine the different programs to best fit their needs while others draw up their own workouts themselves. But if you do need help we are here for you.
